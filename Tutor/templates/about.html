Easy Ace is a Tuition Service Provider founded by a group of students from NUS and NTU, who wish to provide high quality tuition to the students in Singapore. We treat tuition as a service instead of just a business. As we have been through Singapore’s education system ourselves, we know the pressure and difficulty it imposes on each student. Therefore, we aim to develop the most people-oriented service to make every tutor enjoy teaching and every student enjoy studying.

  • Our Vision
  • To become an extraordinary world-leading service provider

  • Our Core Values
  • Passion, Integrity, Accountability, Professionalism
