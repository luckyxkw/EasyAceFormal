FAQ for Students

1. Q: How do I obtain tuition service from Easy Ace?

A: You can simply register by completing Student Registration Form.

2. Q: Can I get tuition without filling registration form?

A: Yes, you can leave your phone number on our webpage a nd we will contact you shortly.

3. Q: How do you charge?

A: Please refer to Tuition Rates

4. Q: Is there a trial lesson? What if I find the first allocated tutor unsuitable for me?

A: Yes , there is one trial lesson for each tuition case. If th e first tutor allocated is unsuitable for you after the first lesson, we are willin g to change the tutor for you. The first lesson conducted will not be charged. However, if you are satisfied with the first tutor and decide to continue , the first lesson will be chargeable .

5. Q: What if I find the second allocated tutor still unsuitable for me?

A: This is an unlikely situation. However, you can still request to change for another tutor from Easy Ace, but the lesson given by the second tuto r remains chargeable.

6. Q: What is the Feedback Form for?

A: Filling a Feedback Form only takes 2 minutes . It is an assessment for the lesson you just had.

7. Q: Why should I fill in the Feedback Form?

A: Feedbacks will allow us to improve our service for you and give due credit/punishment to your tutor. If you complete the feedbacks for at least one consecutive mo nth, vouchers with approximately S$20 value will be awarded. The details of the vouchers are shown in the Welfare.

8. Q: What should I do if I want to reschedule, cancel or add a tuition session?

A: Student can settle with his or her own tutor. But within the first month, you must acknowledge us through email or phone call for any cancellation or a ddition of lesson.

9. Q: How do I contact Easy Ace to get further assistance?

A: Student can email us at tuition@easyace.sg , or directly contact our Student Manager at 82654707.
