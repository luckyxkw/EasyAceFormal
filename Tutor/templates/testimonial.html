What Makes Easy Ace Your Best Choice as a Student!

Why is Easy Ace the Best Choice for You as a Tutor?

Easy Ace aims to build a group of passionate, professional and top-class tutors in Singapore. We provide resources that help making your teaching experience easy, interesting and fruitful.

Enjoyable Teaching Experience

You will be given access to all the teaching materials you may need through our Sharing System. This will significantly lessen your preparation time.

You are able to track, record and review all of the lessons in our feedback and teaching record system. This gives you convenience in arranging and planning your courses.

Great Incentives and Benefits

Easy Ace has a just and fair Star Rating System for the tutors. We value the time and efforts you have put

We will pay our tutors according to their star rating. The higher the star rating is, the higher the commission you will receive.

In addition to your regular tuition pay, you can earn Ace Coins via keying in Teaching Record and redeem vouchers, membership discounts or other benefits with Ace Coins.

Free Training Session

If you are a rookie tutor, Easy Ace is the best suit for you. We will offer you a free training session, which will help you kick-off your career as a tutor.

High Quality Management

Most of Easy Ace’s managing members have been tuition teachers. Therefore, we are the ones who understand your needs and feelings. In Easy Ace, we are open to suggestions and advices. Your voices will certainly be heard by us.

Easy Ace will never change your students without valid reasons from both the tutors and the students.

Easy Ace will recommend students which best suit your requirements on teaching subjects, locations, and time.

Flexible Teaching Schedule

Even if you are not able to teach the scheduled lesson, Easy Ace will back you up with a back-up tutor upon student's request as long as you have notified us before the stipulated time.

Your teaching time is flexible. It is reasonable to alter the lesson timing based on you and your students needs. We will help you in contacting with the students and arrange the timing with them.

All in all, Easy Ace will definitely bring you the best teaching experience!
